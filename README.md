### Predictive Modeling for High Impact Active Learning Classrooms

[Arxiv preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.14335)

We used previously published data [1, 2, 3] to train a model that maps class time spent on lecture (Lec), 
group worksheets (WG), group clicker questions (CG), and student questions (SQ) as measured by COPUS [4] codes to student learning in those classes.

To train this model and/or view the outputs, see `regression.ipynb`


[1] Connell, G. L., Donovan, D. A., & Chambers, T. G. 2016, CBE—Life Sciences Education, 15, ar3
[2] Sundstrom, M., Gambrell, J., Green, C., Traxle, A. L., & Brewe, E. 2025, Relative benefits of different active
learning methods to conceptual physics learning, arXiv:2505.04577 [physics]
[3] Weir, L. K., Barker, M. K., McDonnell, L. M., et al. 2019, PLOS ONE, 14, e0220900
[4] Smith, M. K., Jones, F. H. M., Gilbert, S. L., & Wieman, C. E. 2013, CBE—Life Sciences Education, 12, 618
